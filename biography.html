About!

Star point guard who was named NBA MVP while leading the Golden State Warriors to the league's best record and an NBA Championship in 2015. He set the NBA record for most three-pointers made in a season with 286 during his MVP campaign. Prior to his NBA career, he led Davidson University to the Elite 8 in the NCAA tournament as a sophomore in 2008.
Stephen Curry has a 3 year old daughter named Riley Curry who was born July 19, 2012 in Charolette North Carolina. About a decade before Riley was born, her parents met at a church youth group.
